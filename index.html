Hello!

My name is Alessandro Di Stefano.

I currently work as a UX-Designer at Sunrise in Zurich and as a Lecturer at Schule für Gestaltung Zürich. I have many years of experience in Telecommunications, UX Design & Testing and Game Design.

I can develop user flows, build Design Mockups and test planned solutions before implementation, however my pastime projects involve coding C# in Unity 3D or modelling virtual worlds in Blender 3D.

I graduated from Zurich University of the Arts with a Masters degree in Interaction / Game Design and also graduated from Zurich University of Applied Sciences with a MAS Diploma in Customer Relationship Management.
